Product Lines

Marley Roofing manufactures and sells pitched roofing systems. The Building Research Establishment (BRE) awarded their tiles and slates an "A" Rating, the highest possible environmental rating. Their products include the following below.

Marley Roofing's History

Marley Roofing Company is headquartered in Great Britain. In the 1920s the Aisher family of Kent began a small business building homes. In 1924 they began producing roofing tiles. Initially they were for their own use, but they soon were selling extras to other builders. Within ten years they owned eight concrete tile factories. In 1999 the global company Etex Group purchased Marley.

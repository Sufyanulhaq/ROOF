Vande Heigh Raleigh Shingles

<& /comp/submit_review_widget.mas,brand_name => "Vande Heigh Raleigh", equip_type => "shingle" &>

Vande Heigh Raleigh produces high-end concrete roofing tiles in their Wisconsin manufacturing plant. Each tile is hand-crafted. Their tiles work especially well in intense climate conditions, as they don't degrade. They are fire resistant with a Class A (highest) fire rating. According to the company, their product meets or exceeds industry standards for durability, wind, moisture absorption, and weight. They have a number of styles, including:

<& /comp/google_experiment.mas &>

Custom Brushed -- Surfaces are coated by hand and feature antiquing, moisture marking, and weathering. The surfaces are slightly damaged in order to artistically reflect light.

Shake -- Roughly finished with raw edges to simulate the look of a wooden shake. Custom raking further enhances the wood-like appearance.

Cotswold -- This is a classic model that replicates the roofs seen on manor houses and English churches. The desired appearance is achieved by dimpling and varying the colors in each tile.

High Barrel Tile -- This tile is seen most often in the southwest in the traditional adobe clay color, but can be treated in a variety of colors.

Riviera -- The Riviera mimics the style of Mediterranean roofs continually exposed to sunlight.

English Shingle -- This small tile, available in smooth or textured surfaces, is used for complete roofing or to accessorize specific areas such as bowed windows or additions.

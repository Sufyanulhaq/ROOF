Custom Bilt Metal (CBM) offers several types of metal roofing shingles. The Vail Titan Select is made from heavy-gauge steel, while the Vail Majestic Copper Shingle is manufactured of solid copper, while the Country Manor shakes are made from aluminum. A full line of classically styled standing seam roofing products also is available.

The company claims that its metal roofing shingles hold up in heavy weather conditions, such as heavy snow or high winds. It also claims that these products don't require maintenance.

Corporate History

CBM began as a distributor of rain gutter products in 1974. Founded in southern California, the company expanded across the western United States for the next 15 years. In 1990 the company became active in the metal roofing shingle and shake market.

Warranty

CBM offers a pro-rated lifetime coating warranty for residential products. The warranty extends only to the original homeowner, with completed registration of the product, but guarantees that the paint will not peel, check, chip or crack, or fade or change color within the first five years. For commercial roofing products, the company offers a 30-year limited warranty.

Always carefully read any warranty until you fully understand the conditions.

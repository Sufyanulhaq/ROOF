EDCO Products Inc. manufactures steel shingles under the brand ArrowLine. EDCO produces several types of permanent exterior products, including steel siding, fascia, vinyl siding, shingles and other products designed for residential homes and commercial structures. EDCO describes itself as "largest manufacturer of residential steel roofs and siding in the U.S."

A Summary of EDCO Products

ArrowLine metal shingles are available in two different styles: "Shakes" shingles offer a traditional look, while "slate" shingles provide a European look for buildings. Both types of shingles are rated to withstand winds up to 160 mph and, according to the manufacturer, have the highest rated hail-resistance available. The shingles are available with embossed patterns and a Kynar 500 finish. Slate shingles are available in 11 colors and shake shingles come in four colors. The products hold an Energy Star rating for energy efficiency.

History of EDCO and ArrowLine Metal Shingles

EDCO Products was founded in 1946 in Hopkins, Minn. The company began manufacturing insulated asphalt siding for new homes after World War II and has grown to produce a full line of permanent exterior products. The company notes that it has received several awards from Metal Roofing Magazine, including its Top 10 Product award and its Metal of Honor. EDCO states that the company's focus is on innovation and a "commitment to excellence."

EDCO Warranty Information

Both types of EDCO ArrowLine metal shingles carry a full lifetime warranty. The company states that this warranty is not prorated and can be transferred to a new owner. In addition, the warranty provides fade protection for finish and color, as well as hail protection in the event of damage.

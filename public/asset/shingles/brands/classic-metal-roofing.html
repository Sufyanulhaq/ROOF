Classic Metal Roofing Systems

Classic Metal Roofing Systems offers metal interlocking roofing systems designed to provide energy efficiency. The company claims that the shingles are water-tight and resistant to wind blow-offs and the weight of accumulated snowfalls.

The shingles have a Kynar/Hylar coating that consists of pigments designed to be more sun-reflective.

History

Classic Metal Roofing began in 1980, when founder Don Miller acquired the Rustic shingle from Kaiser Aluminum and Classic Metal Roofing Systems. The company expanded its initial focus on commercial buildings to include roofing products for homes as well. The company is a division of Isaiah Industries Inc. in Piqua, Ohio.

Product Lines

The company's high-end product is named Rustic Shingle, a metal shingle designed to look like expensive wood shakes. Other products include Country Manor Shake, which offers the look of wood shakes; GrandeTile, which mimics barrel tile roofs; the Oxford shingle, for the aesthetics of architectural shingles; and ClickLock premium standing seam for homeowners who like the look of a standing seam.

Warranty

Classic Metal Roofing claims its roofs will last a lifetime and may lower summer air conditioning bills by as much as 20 percent. The warranty guarantees protection from chips, peeling, flakes, blisters, burns, rust, splits or crack as a result of age. Paint systems further hold 30-year limited warranty. Warranties are transferable to new owners up to 40 years after installation. More warranty information is available from local dealers.
